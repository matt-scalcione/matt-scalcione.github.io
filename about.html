About Us

eSports Betting Tips was created by a team of passionate gamers and analysts who saw a gap in the market for high-quality, data-driven coverage of League of Legends and Dota 2 matches. Our mission is to provide bettors with accurate information and thoughtful analysis to help them make informed decisions – without the hype and noise of traditional tout services.

We believe that responsible gaming is the key to enjoying eSports wagering. While we offer opinions and suggestions, we urge all visitors to gamble responsibly and within their means. This site is strictly for entertainment and informational purposes only.
